Best Movies recommended by Miller

Top 3 Asian Action Movies

IP Man (All Series)

This series is a must watch for all the action head. It tells history, romance and beautifully choreographed fighting scene.

Flash Point

Another classic by Donnie Yen. Hair-raising fighting scene will leave you breathless and I lost count how many times I rewatched the fight in the downtown wet market.

Police Story (All Series)

It would be a crime to not mentioned Jacky Chan's masterpiece in this list. Need no introduction (mic drop).
